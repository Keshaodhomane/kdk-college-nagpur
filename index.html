Welcome to KDK College of Engineering, Nagpur

Since its founding KDK College of Engineering has emphasized its strengths in engineering, science and technology to train men and women to become leaders.

Since its founding KDK College of Engineering has emphasized its strengths in engineering, science and technology to train men and women to become leaders. In little over a century, KDK Group has transformed itself into a large, comprehensive institution committed to excellence in education, research and service to the engineering society and to the broader community. Although much has changed, the original mission of the university still rings true today.
